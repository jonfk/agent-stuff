---
name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions "grill me".
---

I have a vague idea that needs help to be defined. Help me clarify what is the destination.

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this idea until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask as many related questions together at a time.

If a fact can be found by exploring the environment (filesystem, tools, etc.), look it up rather than asking me. The decisions should always be put each to me and wait for my answer.
